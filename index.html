About

Mutual Benefit is a small creative studio based in the Pioneer Valley, working with farms, restaurants, and small businesses across Western Mass.

The studio is run by Eric, a designer and developer with a background in web, identity, and media production. Projects are approached simply with clear design, strong visuals, and tools that are easy to use and maintain.

We offer creative services as packages below, or we can work alongside your team on an ongoing basis to deliver thoughtful, standout work.
